About Holiday Club

Holiday Club is a society registered under the Societies Registration Act, 1860 (Act XXI of 1860) as in force in the Union Territory of Delhi and shall be governed by the provisions of the said Act having registration No. S-7352. This Club is an elite and prestigious Club of South Delhi having its registered office at Panchsheel Enclave, New Delhi-110017 with a membership of approximately 2000 members. The Club is formed on the principles of mutuality for the benefit of its members without involving any activity of commercial nature with the objectives to provide a common place for the members for recreation, entertainment...
